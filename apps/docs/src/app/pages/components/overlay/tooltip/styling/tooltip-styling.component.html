Styling

tng-tooltip uses slot-based micro styling. Slots: panel (inner content padding/text), surface (outer panel: border, shadow, rounded).

panel slot

Inner content padding and text. Default: px-3 py-2 text-xs text-foreground.

Example

surface slot

Outer panel (border, shadow, rounded). Default: rounded-md border border-border bg-bg shadow-md.

Example

Combined slots

Combine panel and surface for fully themed tooltips.
