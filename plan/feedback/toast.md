# Toast

Notification system.

## Overview

Short-lived messages that appear (e.g. top-right) to confirm actions or show errors. Auto-dismiss or dismiss button; optional queue and stacking.

## Key points

- Content (text or template); optional title, action, and duration.
- Accessible: live region (polite or assertive); optional close button.
- Composes with container/service; optional position and limit.
