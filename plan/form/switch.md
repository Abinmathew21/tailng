# Switch

Two-state on/off control.

## Overview

Toggle control optimized for binary settings (e.g. enable/disable). Often used in settings panels or forms.

## Key points

- Checked/unchecked; optional labels for on/off.
- Keyboard: Space toggles; focus visible.
- Composes with form and label; can be controlled or uncontrolled.
