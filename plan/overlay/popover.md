# Popover

Trigger semantics and dismissal.

## Overview

Floating panel anchored to a trigger (e.g. button or input). Used for pickers, custom dropdowns, or extra content without full modal behavior.

## Key points

- Position relative to trigger; optional arrow; click outside or Escape to close.
- No mandatory focus trap (unlike dialog); can contain form controls or list.
- Composes with menu, listbox, or custom content.
