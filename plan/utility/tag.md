# Tag

Compact status/count labels.

## Overview

Small label or badge for status (e.g. “New”, “Draft”), category, or count. Often used in lists, filters, or chips.

## Key points

- Text or short content; optional icon and removable (close).
- Variants for status (success, warning, etc.) or neutral.
- Composes with list, filter bar, or input (chips).
