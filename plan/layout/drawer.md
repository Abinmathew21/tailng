# Drawer

Slide-in overlay panel.

## Overview

Panel that slides in from a side (e.g. left or right), often for navigation or detail view. Can overlay or push content; backdrop optional.

## Key points

- Position (start/end/top/bottom); optional backdrop and close on outside click.
- Focus trap and Escape to close; accessible focus management.
- Composes with list, form, or custom content.
