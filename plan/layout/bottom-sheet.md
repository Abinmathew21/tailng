# Bottom Sheet

Partial-height overlay sheet.

## Overview

Panel that slides up from the bottom (e.g. on mobile for actions or details). Can be full height, half, or custom; often dismissible by drag or tap outside.

## Key points

- Bottom-anchored; optional height (full, half, auto).
- Backdrop and gesture to dismiss; focus trap when open.
- Composes with list, buttons, or form.
